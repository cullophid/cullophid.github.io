 Andreas

I am a contractor with extensive experience with web technologies. I am from Denmark, but I have lived in London since March 2012.

In 2014 I co-founded Sentia Analytics. Sentia Analytics analyses retail stores in order to optimize profit margins. By applying image analysis to CCTV camera feeds we collect data on customer movement. We then combine this with point-of-sales data to provide a holistic analysis of the stores performance. Because Sentia is a part-time project, I have had to ensure that the project is state of the art when it comes to automation. The deployment for the sentia dashboard is fully automated, and every commit goes through a vigorous automated testing pipeline before being deployed to production.

One achievement I am particularly proud of is my work at Inxite. While working there we rewrote their entire application using Node.js and MongoDB. It was still early in my career but considering I was the most experienced javascript developer on staff, I got to take the lead in architecting the new product. During this process I had to sample dozens of web frameworks and server libraries to assemble our software stack. I was also tasked with writing apps for both Android and iPhone.

In 2012 I wrote my Masters thesis on machine learning in intelligent buildings. The thesis investigated the possibilities of integrating machine learning algorithms into modern home control systems.

From 2010 to 2011 I co-founded a startup company called jollytime.dk. Though jollytime.dk never became a financial success, I acquired plenty of useful experience.
